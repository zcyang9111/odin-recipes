Fried Chicken

Description

There's nothing like a crispy piece of fried chicken. Whether you like it spicy, mild, or a little sweet, fried chicken is a classic comfort food that's hard to resist.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oil in a deep-fryer or large heavy skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium size bowl, mix together the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Dip each piece of chicken in the egg wash, and then coat the chicken in the flour mixture. Repeat this process with all the chicken pieces.
  4. Fry the chicken in the preheated oil. Cook the chicken in batches, if necessary, to avoid overcrowding the skillet. Cook until the chicken is golden brown and the internal temperature is 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
